About The Symposium

               The ultimate intent of the Symposium is to furnish a common podium to bring all the great minds in and around the locality under one roof and flaunt their expertise. This experience embarks a new beginning to the future minds to reap the intellectual benefits from their peers and mentors. Participants can also make an understanding of the relevant works in relation to others. This will surely be a highbrow treat to every techie geek out there. The department of Information Technology holds privilege in hosting and organizing this Symposium. Dexterity with our devised organizing and the upper hand in management will surely keep the participants exuberant and enthusiastic. Let alone stand the resourceful infrastructure and our instantaneous manoeuvring. Not to mention our adroit mentors and designated coordinators. Hope the participants have an amazeballs experience and a quality time spent with us. Mazel Tov!
